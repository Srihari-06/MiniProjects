Welcome to Spice of South, a premier restaurant located in the heart of Andhra Pradesh, India. We specialize in authentic South Indian cuisine, offering a delightful variety of dishes that capture the true essence of Southern flavors. Whether you're craving the spicy richness of Andhra-style biryani, the comfort of freshly steamed idlis, or the crispy goodness of dosas, our menu has something to satisfy every palate.
